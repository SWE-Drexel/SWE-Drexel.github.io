Membership

Hello! My name is Maureen Burnside and I am the Membership Chair for Drexel SWE. My goal for SWE is to bring engineers together and create a community with a friendly atmosphere. That all starts with you, the member!

If you are new to SWE, the first thing to do is follow us on Facebook and on Dragon link. This will let you know about our upcoming events and also subscribe you to our newsletter. Sometimes events only appear on Facebook or the newsletter so follow both!

Next thing you should do is join National SWE. It's never too early to start thinking about your professional life after college; joining National SWE will give you a jumpstart with exclusive seminars, job search, professional development tools, discounts to certain SWE events, and a special scholarship application which, with a single form, will apply you to over 200 scholarships! You'll also be joining a community that's almost 30,000 members strong.

Instructions to join National SWE are below the page

Finally, come to our events! SWE hosts a variety of events, in areas such as professional development, wellness, outreach, and of course, general membership, so there is surely something to be of interest to you. New members are always welcome to these events! It's never too late to get involved. Get involved with SWE, and open the doors to a great community, awesome events and fantastic opportunities. And don't be afraid to say hi!

How to Become a National Member

To see more detailed instructions with pictures, download the help document here.

1. Go to www.swe.org. On the homepage there is a side tab labeled “JOIN”. Click side tab.

2. Click “JOIN NOW!”

3. Tab will open in a new window. Click the link labeled “Become a member (nonmember).

4. Fill out the form with name, email, etc.

5. Fill out the longer form on the next page.

6. Choose the package membership you would like. Collegiate to Career membership is suggested because with the 5yr program you save the most money.

7. Select the Drexel Chapter (E058).

8. Put in your credit card info and you’re home free.
