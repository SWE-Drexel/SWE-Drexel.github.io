Wellness

SWE is a full supporter of all members to have sound mind and body. SWE incorporates this notion into the organization by implementing events with a theme of wellness. In the past, these have consisted of a Hiking/Cliff Jumping trip, Healthy Living Speaker Event, an intramural sport every term, a yoga class, study sessions during finals and midterms, and 5K runs that have Drexel SWE teams. In the future, there shall be several more varieties of events including mental and physical wellness. Keep a look out for upcoming events and keep your mind open to try new things!

Every “Wellness Wednesday”, there is a post pertaining to wellness! Watch out for updates!

Stay healthy,
Lexi Kapusta
