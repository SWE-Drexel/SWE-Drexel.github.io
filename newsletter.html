Public Relations Committee

What is Public Relations?

The public relations committee of Society of Women Engineers at Drexel University aims to visualize the ideal representation of how women can make a difference in engineering. Our close collaboration with membership, outreach, professional development, networking, and wellness committees has positively influenced the way the larger community at Drexel sees our organization. We hope to advance even further by promoting Drexel SWE events and establishing a presence beyond the campus. Together we can truly impact how everyone sees women in engineering.

Anna
Public Relations Director
