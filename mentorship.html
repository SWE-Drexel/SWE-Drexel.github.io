Mentorship

Welcome to Drexel University’s Society of Women Engineers:

Mentorship Programs!

Creating Professional Networks between Freshman, Upperclassmen, and Alumni.

Drexel SWE mentorship programs are designed to provide female students with the resources they need to tackle the hardcore engineering curriculum/life and become professional working women. Qualified mentors are chosen based on their commitment to nurture their mentees as they fly through this stage of their lives. Mentors draw from their lives experiences, choices, and skill sets to provide proper guidance and feedback to their mentees. They do not simply give advice. They inspire their mentees and ignite that fire within them to achieve any goal.

The Freshman Mentorship Program

A first year college engineering student’s life may seem daunting and overwhelming. Trying to manage the new responsibilities that come with living away from home, taking difficult classes, and adjusting to a rigorous and fast-paced ten week quarter system is not easy. Keeping this in mind, the Drexel SWE Networking Committee developed the Freshman Mentorship Program in 2013, with hopes of providing Drexel first year and transfer females with a smooth transition from high school to college. Each student is paired one-on-one ith an upper classmen with a similar major or professional goal. This allows the mentor to answer any questions and guide her mentee down the right path towards success. The Alumni-Student Mentorship Program

This program builds professional relations and networks between Drexel SWE upper classmen and Drexel SWE alumni. This gives current Drexel students the opportunity to connect with alumni who have excelled in their careers in industry, research, and academia. Likewise, this allows alumni to share their professional and academic experiences, and guide upperclassmen as they approach graduation and get ready to enter the real world. At the moment, this program is still at the developmental stage. If any alumni are interested in joining this program, please fill out this form .
